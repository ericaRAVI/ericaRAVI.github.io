About Us

Built on experience. Driven by engineering.

Erica Engineering Works was established in 2021 by two engineers with deep practical and industrial experience. One founder has over 5 years of hands-on expertise in core VFD repair and power electronics, while the other brings more than 20 years of experience across textile, dyeing, and food processing industries.

Our strength lies in engineering-based diagnosis, chip-level repair, and identifying the true root cause of failures to prevent repeat breakdowns and production losses.

  • ✔ VFD repair – all brands & capacities
  • ✔ Power electronics & control card repair
  • ✔ Root cause failure analysis
  • ✔ Transparent and reliable repair process
