About Erica Engineering Works

Engineering depth, practical experience, and long-term reliability

Who We Are

Erica Engineering Works was established in 2021 by two qualified engineers with a clear mission: to provide engineering-driven, reliable industrial solutions instead of temporary repairs.

One founder brings over 5 years of hands-on experience in core VFD repair and power electronics, while the other founder contributes more than 20 years of practical industrial experience across textile, dyeing, and food processing industries.

This strong combination of electronics expertise and real industry exposure allows us to understand not only the equipment, but also the application and operating conditions.

Our Core Strength – VFD Repair

VFD repair is our primary and strongest business. We specialize in all-brand and all-capacity variable frequency drives with complete chip-level diagnostics.

  • ✔ Control card, power card, SMPS & IGBT repair
  • ✔ Detailed fault analysis instead of board replacement
  • ✔ Root cause identification to prevent repeat failures
  • ✔ Load testing before dispatch

Strong Component Inventory

To reduce turnaround time and production loss, we maintain a ready stock of commonly used semiconductor and power electronics components.

  • ✔ IGBTs, MOSFETs & power modules
  • ✔ Gate drivers, ICs & control components
  • ✔ Capacitors, rectifiers & protection devices
  • ✔ Cooling fans, sensors & auxiliary parts

This inventory enables faster repair, better reliability, and reduced dependency on external supply delays.
