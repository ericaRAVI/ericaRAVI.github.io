About Erica Engineering Works

Engineering-driven solutions built on experience, expertise, and integrity

Our Journey

Erica Engineering Works was established in 2021 by two qualified engineers with a shared vision of delivering reliable, engineering-based industrial solutions.

One of the founders brings over 5 years of hands-on experience in core VFD repair and power electronics, while the other founder contributes more than 20 years of practical industry experience across diverse sectors including textiles, dyeing, and food processing.

Both founders are well qualified and trained at reputed institutes of India, combining academic strength with real-world industrial exposure.

Our Core Expertise

  • ✔ VFD Repair – All brands & all capacities
  • ✔ Chip-level power electronics repair
  • ✔ Root cause failure analysis
  • ✔ Power quality analysis & energy audit
  • ✔ Panel erection & industrial electrical services

Our Engineering Philosophy

We strongly believe that reliable repair begins with correct diagnosis. Our approach is strictly engineering-based — not trial and error.

By identifying the true root cause of failures, we help industries prevent repeat breakdowns, reduce downtime, and minimize production losses. Transparency and technical clarity remain at the core of our repair process.
