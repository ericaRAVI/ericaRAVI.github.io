VFD & AC Drive Repair – Our Core Expertise

VFD failures are often caused by internal component degradation and external power quality problems. At Erica Engineering Works, we focus on root cause analysis rather than temporary fixes.

  • Chip-level repair of SMPS, DC bus capacitors, IGBT modules, and control cards
  • Resolution of faults such as no display, DC bus over-voltage, over-current, and repeated tripping
  • Support for all major VFD brands used in Indian industries
  • Correlation of drive failures with harmonics, voltage imbalance, and regenerative energy

Our approach ensures improved drive reliability and reduced repeat failures.
