VFD Repair is Our Core Expertise

We specialize exclusively in multi-brand, all-capacity VFD repair with deep technical diagnostics and root cause failure analysis — not just replacement.

Why Our VFD Repair is Different

At Erica Engineering Works, VFD repair is not a side service — it is our core business. We analyze why a drive failed, not only how to make it run again. This approach prevents repeat failures and saves valuable production time.

  • ✔ All brands & all power ratings (low to high capacity)
  • ✔ Chip-level diagnostics & component-level repair
  • ✔ IGBT, power card, control card & SMPS repair
  • ✔ Root cause analysis to avoid repeated breakdowns
  • ✔ Reduced downtime & improved equipment life

Our VFD Repair Process

1. Detailed Inspection

Physical inspection, fault history review, and application understanding to identify stress factors and operating conditions.

2. Advanced Diagnostics

Electrical testing, waveform analysis, and card-level troubleshooting to pinpoint exact failure points.

3. Precision Repair

Replacement of defective components using quality parts with strict repair standards.

4. Load Testing

Functional and load testing under controlled conditions to ensure stable and reliable performance.

5. Root Cause Report

Clear explanation of failure reason with preventive recommendations to avoid future production loss.
