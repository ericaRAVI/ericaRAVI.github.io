VFD & AC Drive Repair Services in Punjab

Erica Engineering Works provides specialized VFD and AC drive repair services for industrial plants across Punjab. We focus on chip-level repair and detailed root cause analysis to ensure long-term reliability of your drives.

Common VFD Problems We Repair

  • No display / dead drive
  • DC bus over-voltage or under-voltage faults
  • IGBT short or gate driver failure
  • SMPS and auxiliary power supply issues
  • Over-current, ground fault, and repeated tripping

Our VFD Repair Process

  1. Detailed fault diagnosis and visual inspection
  2. Component-level testing of power and control circuits
  3. Replacement of failed electronic components
  4. Load testing and parameter verification
  5. Onsite commissioning support (if required)

Power Quality & Root Cause Analysis

Many VFD failures are linked to poor power quality. Our team evaluates harmonics, voltage imbalance, regenerative energy, and earthing issues to prevent repeat drive failures.
